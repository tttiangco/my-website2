Streamers within the community

Pictured here are three of some of the most influential and popular Valorant streamers known, in order from left to right is Tarik, TenZ, and Sinatraa. All of these people are professional players and are active within the community. A large majority of the pros used to be professionals/semi-professionals in Counter Strike: Global Offensive, which played a role in their transition to Valorant's esport scene.

Valorant also has a very active Esports scene, with hundreds of thousands of community led tournaments, as well as official events called "Masters Tournaments". Every year there are three different sections within a Valorant Esports season where top ranked teams in each major region fight to claim a spot for international LAN tournaments, usually hosted in different countries every year.
